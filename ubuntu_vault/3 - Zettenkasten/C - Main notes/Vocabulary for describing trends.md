# best verbs to describe trends
1. Double: to become, or make something become, twice as much or as many
	Examples: Membership almost doubled in two years
	Triple, Quadruple, multiply
2. Bounce back: to return to their previous high level or value after a period of difficulty
	= recover 
3. swell: to increase or make something increase in number or size
	Examples: Spending on transport swelled to 20 thousand US dollars
	= escalate
4. halve: to reduce by a half
	Example: the shares have halved in value
5. Shrink: to become or to make something smaller in size of amount
	Example: The market for their products is shrinking 
	= Lessen = Diminish = Lower
6. Collapse: to decrease suddenly in amount or value
	Example: Share prices collapsed after news of poor trading figures
7. Plateau: To stay at a steady level after a period of growth or progress 
	Example: Unemployment has at last plateaued out
