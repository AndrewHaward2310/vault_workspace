## 2. Adverbs
- Adverbs of approximate
	- about
	- around
	- approximately
	- roughly
- Adverbs describe data which lower than figure:
	- nearly
	- just under
	- well under
	- lower than
	- below
-  Adverbs describe data which higher than figure:
	- just over
	- well over
	- higher than
## 3. Units:
- Format: 
	- 10.000.000
	- 12.5%
- You say a, one, two, etc. thousand without a final "s" on "thousand"
- Thousands (of ...) can be used if there is no number or quantity before it 
- Always use a plural verb with thousand or thousands, expect when an amount of money is mentioned:
	- Four thousand (people) are expected to attend 
	- Two thousand (dollars) was withdrawn from account
