# **S : The amount of + Noun + Relative Clause**
# 1. the unit is a percentage 
- percentage + countable /uncountable nouns
- proportion + countable /uncountable nouns
- rate + countable /uncountable nouns
	- Meaning: a measurement of the speed/the number of times something happens
	- Example: Crime rate, birth rate, fertility rate, mortality rate, death rate, unemployment rate, success rate ...
	- The immigration rate to the UK because of definite jobs 
- ratio
	- Meaning: the relationship between two groups of people or things that is represented by two numbers showing how much larger one group is than the other
	- Ex1: The ratio of applications to available places currently stands at 100:1
	- Ex2: The school has a very high teacher-student ratio
# 2. kilo, dollars, pounds, ...
- quantity + countable/uncountable nouns
- amount + uncountable nouns
- number + countable nouns
# 3. Some words which describe data
- Production
- Consumption
- Income
- Earnings
- Salary
- Sales
- Spending
# 4. The/this figure
- Describe the data which used in the previous
- Ex: The amount of petrol and oil which were consumed in 1980 was 35 quadrillion units, which was highest.
	- This figure then declined slightly in the following five years to about 30 quadrillion units 
	- The figure for natural gas was much lower, at 20 quadrillion units in the same year
