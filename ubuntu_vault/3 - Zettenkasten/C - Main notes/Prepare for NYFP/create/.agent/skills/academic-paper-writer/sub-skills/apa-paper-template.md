# APA Paper Template (7th Edition)

## APA Format for Research Papers

### Page Layout
- **Font**: Times New Roman 12pt, or Calibri 11pt, or Arial 11pt
- **Spacing**: Double-spaced throughout
- **Margins**: 1 inch all sides
- **Page numbers**: Top right, starting from title page
- **Running head**: Shortened title (≤50 chars), ALL CAPS, top left

### APA Citation Format
```
In-text (parenthetical): (Author, Year)
In-text (narrative):      Author (Year)
Multiple authors:         (Author & Author, Year)
3+ authors:               (First Author et al., Year)
Direct quote:             (Author, Year, p. XX)
Multiple citations:       (Author, Year; Author, Year)

Journal:    Author, A. A., & Author, B. B. (Year). Title of article. 
            Title of Periodical, Volume(Issue), pp–pp. 
            https://doi.org/xxxxx

Book:       Author, A. A. (Year). Title of work: Capital letter also 
            for subtitle (Edition). Publisher.

Chapter:    Author, A. A. (Year). Title of chapter. In E. E. Editor (Ed.),
            Title of book (pp. xx–xx). Publisher.

Website:    Author, A. A. (Year, Month Day). Title of page. Site Name. 
            https://www.url.com
```

### Key APA 7th Edition Rules
1. Use **et al.** for 3+ authors from the first citation
2. Hanging indent for references (0.5 inch)
3. DOI as hyperlink format: https://doi.org/xxxxx
4. No "Retrieved from" for most online sources
5. Include issue numbers for all journals (even continuous pagination)
6. Level 1 headings: Centered, Bold, Title Case
7. Level 2 headings: Left-Aligned, Bold, Title Case
8. Level 3 headings: Left-Aligned, Bold Italic, Title Case
