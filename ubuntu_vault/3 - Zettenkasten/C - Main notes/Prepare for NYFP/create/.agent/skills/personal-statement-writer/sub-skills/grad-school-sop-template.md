# Graduate School Statement of Purpose Template

## SOP for Masters/PhD Applications (1-2 pages)

### Structure

```
PARAGRAPH 1: RESEARCH INTEREST & MOTIVATION (4-5 sentences)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
State your research area clearly. What specific problem interests 
you? Why? Connect to a concrete experience or observation.

PARAGRAPH 2: ACADEMIC BACKGROUND (4-5 sentences)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
Relevant coursework, GPA highlights, and academic achievements.
Mention specific courses that prepared you for this research area.

PARAGRAPH 3: RESEARCH EXPERIENCE (5-6 sentences)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
Describe your research projects in detail. What was the problem?
What was YOUR contribution? What were the results? Publications?
Use technical language appropriate for the field.

PARAGRAPH 4: PROFESSIONAL / INDUSTRY EXPERIENCE (3-4 sentences)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
If applicable. Connect industry experience to research interests.
What real-world problems motivate your research?

PARAGRAPH 5: WHY THIS UNIVERSITY & PROGRAM (4-5 sentences)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Name specific professors whose work aligns with yours. Mention 
specific labs, facilities, or research groups. Show genuine fit.

PARAGRAPH 6: CAREER GOALS (3-4 sentences)
─────────────────────────────────────────
Short-term (during the program) and long-term (after graduation).
What kind of researcher/professional do you want to become?

CLOSING: (1-2 sentences)
────────────────────────
Confident, forward-looking conclusion.
```

### SOP vs Personal Statement

| SOP                                | Personal Statement                    |
| ---------------------------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| Research-focused                   | Narrative-focused                     |
| Technical language OK              | Accessible language                   |
| Emphasis on academic fit           | Emphasis on personal growth           |
| Mentions specific faculty          | Mentions broader impact               |
| Required for PhD/research programs | Required for fellowships/scholarships |

### Key Tips
1. Be specific about research interests — vague SOPs are weak
2. Name 2-3 faculty members whose work aligns with yours
3. Show progression: coursework → research → graduate school
4. Don't repeat your CV — add context and motivation
