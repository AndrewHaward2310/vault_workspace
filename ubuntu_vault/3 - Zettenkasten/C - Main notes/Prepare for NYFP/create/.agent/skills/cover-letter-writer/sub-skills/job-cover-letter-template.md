# Job Cover Letter Template

## Template for Job Applications

```
[Your Name]
[Your Address]
[City, Country]
[Email] | [Phone]
[Date]

[Hiring Manager Name or "Hiring Manager"]
[Company Name]
[Company Address]

Dear [Mr./Ms. Last Name / Hiring Manager],

PARAGRAPH 1: THE HOOK (2-3 sentences)
I am writing to apply for the [Position Title] at [Company Name].
[One sentence showing specific knowledge about the company and 
genuine enthusiasm — NOT generic praise].
[One sentence stating your core qualification match].

PARAGRAPH 2: WHY ME — EVIDENCE (4-5 sentences)
In my role as [Current/Previous Title] at [Company], I [specific 
achievement with quantified result]. This experience directly 
prepared me for [specific aspect of the target role]. I also 
[second relevant achievement], demonstrating my ability to 
[skill the job requires].

PARAGRAPH 3: WHY THEM — GENUINE FIT (3-4 sentences)
I am particularly drawn to [Company Name]'s [specific project, 
value, or initiative]. [Show you've researched the company]. 
My experience in [relevant area] aligns with your team's work 
on [specific product/project].

PARAGRAPH 4: CLOSE WITH CONFIDENCE (2-3 sentences)
I would welcome the opportunity to discuss how my [skill set] 
can contribute to [Company's goal]. Thank you for your 
consideration, and I look forward to hearing from you.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
```

### Cover Letter Rules
1. **Never exceed 1 page** — respect the reader's time
2. **Customize EVERY letter** — mention company name, specific role, specific projects
3. **Lead with value** — what can you do for THEM?
4. **Mirror job posting language** — use their keywords
5. **No salary expectations** unless explicitly requested
