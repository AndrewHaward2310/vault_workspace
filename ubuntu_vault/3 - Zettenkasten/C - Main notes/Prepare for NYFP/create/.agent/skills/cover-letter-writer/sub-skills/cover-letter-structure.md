# Cover Letter Structure

## The 4-Part Framework

### Part 1: Opening (WHY I'M WRITING)
- State the position/program clearly
- Show ONE specific piece of knowledge about them
- Keep to 2-3 sentences maximum

### Part 2: Body — Evidence (WHY ME)
- 2-3 concrete achievements relevant to the role
- Use the STAR-Q format: Situation → Task → Action → Result (Quantified)
- Mirror keywords from the posting

### Part 3: Body — Fit (WHY THEM)
- Show genuine research about the organization
- Connect your values/goals to their mission
- Be specific — name projects, teams, or initiatives

### Part 4: Closing (CALL TO ACTION)
- Express enthusiasm without desperation
- Thank them for their time
- State availability for interview/discussion

### Length & Format
- **Maximum 1 page** (300-400 words ideal)
- **4-5 paragraphs** total
- **Margins**: 1 inch
- **Font**: Match your CV/resume font
- **File name**: `LastName_CoverLetter_CompanyName.pdf`
