# BASIC INFORMATION ABOUT IELTS WRITING TASK 2

- Question Type: You will be presented with a specific question or prompt related to a general topic 
- Word Count: You are required to write an essay of at least 250 words. Going below the word limit can result in a penalty 
- Time limit: You have 40 minutes to complete Task 2
- Your essay will be graded on a scale of 0 to 9, with half-band increments
- Your score is based on several factors:
	- Task Response: How well you address the task prompt 
	- Coherence and Cohesion: How well you organize and connect your ideas
	- Lexical Resource (Vocabulary): Your range and accuracy of vocabulary
	- Grammatical Range and Accuracy: Your use of a variety of sentence structures and the accuracy of grammar 
- Task 2 is worth two thirds of your total writing mark; Task 1 is worth one third
