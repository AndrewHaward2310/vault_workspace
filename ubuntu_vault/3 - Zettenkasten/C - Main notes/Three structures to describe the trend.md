# Three structures to describe the trend
![[Pasted image 20251111222613.png]]
Structure 1: The amount of Co2 which was produced per person in the UK decreased gradually from about 11 to under 9 tonnes between 1967 and 2007

Structure 2: There was a gradual decrease in the amount of CO2 produced per person in the UK from about 11 to under 9 tonnes between 1967 and 2007
There + be + noun phrase + in S + number + time 

Structure 3: The period between 1967 and 2007 witnessed a gradual decrease in the amount of CO2 produced per person in the UK from  about 11 to under 9 tonnes 
time (noun phrase) + verb (witness, record, experience, undergo, ...) + noun phrase + in S + number
