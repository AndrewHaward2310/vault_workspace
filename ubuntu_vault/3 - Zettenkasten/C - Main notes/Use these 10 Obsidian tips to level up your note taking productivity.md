# Use these 10 Obsidian tips to level up your note taking productivity

1. Don't just work On your system
	"There's a difference between working on your note taking system, and using your note taking system to do real work."
	Always know which you're doing 
2. Obsidian is super powerful when you start thinking in systems
3. Build systems based on how you're working
	Don't create a system first and then try to change your behavior to fit the new system
4. Taking notes is easy
5. Productivity ebbs and flows - build systems that supports this
6. Treat it like a living knowledge base 
7. New ideas come from the intersection of existing ideas 
8. Writing is Thinking 
9. Mimicry is a shortcut to success
